# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Two overlapping players, both mobile-first (phone in hand, short sessions on the couch or commute):

1. **Casual collector**: collects and trades Spektrum NFT cards, plays quick PvP matches. Crypto-curious, not crypto-native; the wallet should feel like a game inventory, not a DeFi terminal.
2. **Competitive TCG player**: came for the game. Deck optimization, ladder play, longer sessions. Tolerates the chain; never wants it in the way.

Primary job on any screen: get back to playing, collecting, or trading with minimum friction. Settings, shop, and inventory are pit stops, not destinations.

## Product Purpose

Spektrum is a trading card game with on-chain ownership (Solana, Privy embedded wallets). The client is the whole game surface: matches, collection, deck building, shop, trading. Success: the chrome disappears and the card art carries the visual identity; players describe the app as "fast and fun", never "a crypto app".

## Brand Personality

Playful, energetic, warm. Juicy feedback on actions, friendly copy, momentum in transitions. Energy comes from motion, type, and the orange accent doing real work, not from clutter or noise. Warm dark surfaces, never cold or sterile.

## Visual Identity Constraint

The established palette stays: dark warm-tinted surfaces with the Spektrum orange accent (`#f97316` family). Layout, spacing, hierarchy, and component structure are open for reinvention; the palette is not. Orange is an accent with a job (primary actions, active states, brand moments), not a border color applied to every container.

## Anti-references

- **Crypto-bro neon**: neon green/purple on black, glow effects, degen energy. The old carve-over's orange-glow borders fall in this bucket.
- **Sterile SaaS dashboard**: white-cards-on-gray, shadcn defaults left untouched, admin-panel feel. This is a game.

## Design Principles

1. **Chrome serves the cards.** UI is quiet so card art is loud. One accent doing real work beats five decorations.
2. **Game, not app.** Surfaces should feel like a premium game menu: confident type, rhythm, playful motion, never form-builder defaults.
3. **Wallet is inventory.** Blockchain mechanics dressed in game language; no crypto jargon or DeFi visual tropes.
4. **Juice the action, calm the rest.** Feedback on taps and wins is energetic; passive screens are calm and readable.
5. **Thumb-first.** Mobile is the primary device: bottom-weighted actions, 44px targets, constrained content width (max-w-sm/md), no desktop-stretched layouts.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all text and controls. `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on every animation. Touch targets ≥44px. Orange accent always paired with a second signal (position, icon, label), never color alone.
